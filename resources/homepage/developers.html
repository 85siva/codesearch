About

We - the development team of Codesearch - are a group of students from Austria.
We originally got the idea for Codesearch in 2009 when we were working together with the company Infonova GmbH during a school project.
Infonova was using a self developed program called SVN-Search, which they used company-internal and was pretty much a basic version of todays Codesearch.
It had only very few features and our original task was to extend it. During our school project - which was on for about a year - we added some functionality to SVN-Search, but we were never really satisfied with the result.
The basic design of SVN-Search allowed almost no extensions in any way, so when it was time for us to decide on a topic for our diploma thesis in 2010, we asked the people at Infonova if they would be interested in a new version of SVN-Search with a lot of additional features. However, after a few discussions we realized, that Codesearch could be much more than we can make it into, we would need the help of a community. That's why we decided to make it Open Source, also our main attention during programming was, to make it as extendable as possible.
